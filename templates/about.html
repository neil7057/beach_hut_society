It's all about Beach Huts.

Who doesn't love a beach hut or better still a row of them. Typically wooden and painted in a variety of bright colours.

This site is dedicated to those of us who appreciate this peculiarly British construction.

Dating from Georgian times when the rich and influential went to 'take the waters' and then the victorian era where modesty was the vogue.

There's no specific (polite) name for us fellow BH fanciers, let's just call us Hutters.
